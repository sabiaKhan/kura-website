It All Started Few Years Ago

We offer a wide range of Industrial and Home Automation Solutions, Solar Products, Lighting Applications and Tracking solutions with artificially intelligent servers. We are on a mission to empower the world to build. The Internet of Things today offers limitless possibilities for building solutions to solve business challenges and create amazing customer experiences. Doing so is often extremely difficult due to lack of interoperability. Our goal is to make it easy for everyone to build IoT solutions, lowering the barrier to IoT adoption. We have entered into an unprecedented era in which the consumer's expectation is that household appliances, products and electronic devices communicate bi-directionally with each other without human interaction. At over 8 billion and growing, the number of connected devices now outnumbers the world’s population. Astoundingly, that number is expected to grow exponentially. Company Focus

To acquire market of UPS, Solar Inverters and Chargers by providing a low cost, more reliable, better looking and unique featured product. A lot of efforts have been put on the appearance of the product. Once a reasonable market is acquired and a brand value is created, the company shall move to the next level where new products and services will be added. The company follows the Alan Kay philosophy i.e. People who are really serious about software should make their own hardware. The main focus areas are:

Software

Electrower designs simple yet powerful control algorithms written mostly on C/C++ on microcontrollers from renowned companies like Microchip, Freescale & ST Microelectronics. Own software can lead to customization into the product by which it can be transformed into other application for tenders and custom orders.

Hardware

Electrower designed its own hardware with more advanced and power efficient topology in the control section. All basic to advanced protections all are taken in to consideration while designing the hardware. Improved power layout of combined with protections increases system reliability and robustness in the field. In house R&D reduces overall cost of the system.
